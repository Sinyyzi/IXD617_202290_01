About Me

Hello! My name is Xinyi Shen, and I currently study Interaction & UI/UX design in Academy of Art University in San Francisco. I was born in Hangzhou, China. I studied printmaking there at my university and studied illustration with an MFA in San Francisco. I have a strong sense of empathy and am willing to think about people's psychological and social problems. My works are inspired by nature and the small things around me. I'm also good at visual design and illustrations.
